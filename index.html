Jujutsu Kaisen

Jujutsu Kaisen is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Gege Akutami. The story follows Yuji Itadori, a high school student with extraordinary physical abilities, who gets involved in the world of curses and sorcery after encountering a cursed object.

The anime adaptation of Jujutsu Kaisen has gained widespread popularity for its intense action scenes, well-developed characters, and engaging plot.

Key Information

  • Genre: Action, Dark Fantasy, Supernatural
  • Original Manga: Gege Akutami
  • First Aired: October 3, 2020
  • Episodes: 24 (Season 1)
